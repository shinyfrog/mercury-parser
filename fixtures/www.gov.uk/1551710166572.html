Magistrate advisory committee recruitment information

Details of vacancies and where to apply to be a magistrate in your area.

Published 3 October 2013
Last updated 4 March 2019 — see all updates
From:
Ministry of Justice and HM Courts & Tribunals Service
Applies to:
England and Wales

Documents

Magistrates recruitment information by area

MS Word Document, 885KB

Details

You need to apply to the advisory committee for your local magistrates’ court. Read the document for information about all vacancies and upcoming recruitment in your area, including where you need to apply.

The following areas are currently accepting applications:

  • Birmingham & Solihull
  • Black Country
  • Cambridgeshire
  • Essex
  • Greater Manchester (Criminal)
  • Greater Manchester (Family)
  • London (Central & South)
  • London (North & East)
  • London (West)
  • Norfolk
  • Suffolk
  • Wales - Dyfed Powys - Gwent - Mid & South Glamorgan - North Wales - West Glamorgan

Find out more about how to apply to be a magistrate.
